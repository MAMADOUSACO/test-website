Gravity Settings

Gravity is one of the most fundamental physics parameters that affects how objects move in your simulation. In BallSim, gravity is represented as a vector with direction and magnitude.

// Setting gravity (default is downward at 9.8)
simulation.setGravity({
  x: 0,
  y: 9.8
});

You can adjust the x and y components to change both the direction and strength of gravity. Setting both to zero creates a zero-gravity environment.

Friction and Elasticity

Friction affects how objects slow down when moving along surfaces, while elasticity (also called restitution) determines how bouncy objects are when they collide.

// Setting surface friction (0-1, where 0 is frictionless)
surface.setFriction(0.2);

// Setting elasticity (0-1, where 1 is perfectly bouncy)
ball.setElasticity(0.8);
