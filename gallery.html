Description

A realistic simulation of objects falling into a gravity well with adjustable parameters. This simulation demonstrates the principles of gravitational attraction and orbital mechanics in a simplified 2D environment.

You can adjust the mass of the central object, add new particles, and see how they interact with the gravitational field.
